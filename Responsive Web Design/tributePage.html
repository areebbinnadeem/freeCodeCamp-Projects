Abdul Sattar Edhi

'Father Teresa' of Pakistan

Here's a time line of Abdul Sattar Edhi's Life:

  • 1928 - Born in Bantva Manavadar, British India
  • 1947 - Migrated to Pakistan
  • 1951 - Opened a small pharmacy where he sold medicine for free
  • 1957 - Founded Edhi Trust(a non profit social welfare organization)
  • 1989 - Received Nishan-e-Imtiaz from the Government of Pakistan
  • 2007 - Gandhi Peace Award
  • 2011 - London Peace Award
  • 2016 - Dies at the age of 88

"No Religion is Higher than Humanity"
